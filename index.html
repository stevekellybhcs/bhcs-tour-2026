GRIT Push through, not past
Grit is how you respond in the next 10 seconds, not how you feel.
Looks like
After a mistake — sprint back, next job.
After a bad call — no reaction, reset.
Late in the day — same body language as the morning.
Finishing a drill at full intensity when you're tired.
Doesn't look like
Pushing through injury without speaking up.
Confusing stubbornness with perseverance.
Dropping standards because you're fatigued.
SERVE Team before self
Serve is most visible when no one is watching.
Looks like
Helping pack up when you're tired.
Bringing energy from the bench when you didn't play much.
Saying thank you to refs, drivers, and staff.
Leaving every venue better than you found it.
Doesn't look like
Serving only when it's visible.
Being selfless to the point of not speaking up for yourself.
Confusing being quiet with being humble.
LEAD Step up before you're asked
Leadership is taking responsibility for standards — not just action.
Looks like
Organising your group without being told.
Checking in on a disengaged teammate.
Addressing dropped standards respectfully.
If you mess up — own it immediately.
Doesn't look like
Being the loudest voice.
Correcting others without relationship.
Leading for recognition rather than for the team.

General behaviour
Follow all staff instructions immediately and respectfully.
Stay with your allocated group unless given permission by staff.
Represent BHCS positively in public at all times.
Treat teammates, staff, drivers, and members of the public with respect.
Accommodation
Be in your allocated room by curfew — communicated each evening.
Don't enter other rooms unless approved by staff.
Don't leave accommodation without staff supervision.
Respect all property and facilities.
Travel & safety
Seatbelts on at all times when travelling.
Remain seated while vehicles are moving.
Stay within designated boundaries at all venues.
Beach: sand-based activity and ankle-deep water only unless directed otherwise.
Be on time at all meeting points. Safety directions are not optional.
Devices & social media
Use phones responsibly.
Don't post content that reflects negatively on BHCS, staff, teammates, or venues.
Don't share images or footage of incidents or emergencies.
Surrender devices if directed by staff during an emergency.
Prohibited
Alcohol, vaping, smoking, or drug use.
Leaving accommodation or venues without permission.
Physical aggression, bullying, or harassment.
Deliberate damage to property.
Consequences
Breach of expectations may result in restriction from activities, parent notification, behaviour consequences on return, or immediate removal from tour at parent expense.
